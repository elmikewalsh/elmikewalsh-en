Ode to Whisky

7 September 2019

Some reflexions on that magical liquid called whiskey

Too much of anything is bad, but too much good whiskey is barely enough. - Mark Twain

I like my whisky old and my women young. - Errol Flynn

Whisky is liquid sunshine. - George Bernard Shaw

Always carry a flagon of whiskey in case of snakebite and furthermore always carry a small snake. - W. C. Fields

My God, so much I like to drink Scotch that sometimes I think my name is Igor Stra-whiskey! - Igor Stravinsky
